Featured Project: Sky Spy

Sky Spy is a product of research by HTC. Set the date and time to a future point, and Sky Spy will check the weather at all locations at that time. The map is divided into a grid at 20Km intervals. The data is updated from MET Éireann every hour. MET Éireann's models frequently change their mind; the closer the event date, the higher the accuracy.
